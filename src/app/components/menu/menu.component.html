About

Hello! Thank you for trying out my Star Trek Adventures Captain's Log Assistant (Sta'Cla!)

This app is designed to try reduce the work involved in running your captain's log adventures to spend less time working out the mechanics and more time playing.

If you find any issues or would be interested getting involved in making it better, feel free to drop by the GitHub Repo. You can also find me on reddit as u/GreyScot88

I'm normally a backend guy anyone wanna play with the front end let me know, also wouldnt mind some help creating preset characters and ships as I want to avoid just copying official resources.
